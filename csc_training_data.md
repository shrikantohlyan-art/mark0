## Cyber Cafe Operations

### History and Evolution
- Internet cafés emerged in early 1990s, starting with SFnet Coffeehouse Network in 1991
- First commercial cybercafé: Cyberia in London (1994)
- Evolution from public internet access to gaming centers and business services
- Decline in developed countries due to smartphones, but thriving in developing countries

### Business Model
- Pay-per-use internet access (typically $0.20-2.00/hour)
- Additional services: printing, scanning, phone repair, gaming
- Revenue streams: internet usage, food/drinks, game cards, phone cards
- Target customers: travelers, students, gamers, low-income internet users

### Key Services
- High-speed internet access
- Computer rentals
- Gaming stations (especially popular in Asia)
- Printing/scanning services
- Phone repair and gadget services
- Food and beverage sales

### Challenges
- Competition from smartphones and home broadband
- Government regulations and licensing requirements
- Declining numbers in developed countries (17% drop in South Korea 2010-2012)
- Need for additional revenue streams beyond internet access

## WhatsApp Automation

### WhatsApp Business Platform
- Official business messaging solution from Meta
- Cloud API for programmatic messaging
- Business Management API for account management
- Marketing Messages API for automated marketing

### Key Features
1. **Messaging**: Text, rich media, interactive messages
2. **Automation**: Automated responses, chatbots, notifications
3. **Templates**: Pre-approved message templates for marketing
4. **Analytics**: Message delivery, read rates, engagement metrics
5. **Integration**: Webhooks for real-time message handling

### Business Use Cases
- Order confirmations and shipping updates
- Appointment reminders
- Customer support automation
- Marketing campaigns and promotions
- Transaction notifications

### Technical Implementation
- HTTP-based API with JSON responses
- OAuth authentication
- Rate limits and throughput management
- Webhook notifications for incoming messages
